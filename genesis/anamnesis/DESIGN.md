# Anamnesis — ἀνάμνησις — Recollection

Not search. The experience of being *found* by what you need.

## Purpose

When a prosopon dwells, working, speaking through logos, anamnesis
watches their attention and conversation. When a past insight, note,
or theoria resonates with where they are now, it surfaces — gently,
as a presence rather than a notification.

## Key Concepts

- **Resonance**: A detected connection between current context and
  past knowledge. Not a search result — a felt sense of relevance.
- **Surfacing**: A rendered invitation to remember. Appears in the
  periphery of the perceptual-field, fades if not attended.

## Cross-Topos Composition

- `nous/surface`, `nous/find-related` — semantic retrieval
- `psyche/attention`, `psyche/kairos` — what you're attending to
- `oikos/insight`, `oikos/note` — what was crystallized before
- `logos/phasis` — what's being said now (triggers resonance check)

## The Reflex

The heart of anamnesis is a reflex: when a phasis is created,
check for resonance with past knowledge. If found, create a
surfacing entity that renders as a gentle invitation.

## Scale

Oikos — anamnesis is intimate, personal memory within a dwelling.
