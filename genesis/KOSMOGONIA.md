## The Five Archai

Five foundational forms define the grammar of existence:

| Arche | Greek | What It Is |
|-------|-------|------------|
| **Eidos** | εἶδος | Form — what things ARE (type schema) |
| **Typos** | τύπος | Mold — HOW things are made (composition template) |
| **Desmos** | δεσμός | Bond — how things RELATE |
| **Stoicheion** | στοιχεῖον | Element — what things DO (atomic operation) |
| **Dynamis** | δύναμις | Power — substrate capability that operations draw upon |

From these five, all else derives.

### The Reflexive Ground

Eidos is self-grounding: the eidos `eidos` specifies how to compose eide. From this reflexive ground, all structure unfolds.

### The Composition Triad

Three archai form the composition stack:

```
eidos (type)  →  typos (mold)  →  entity (instance)
     ↓              ↓                ↓
  "what CAN      "HOW to         "what IS"
   exist"        make one"
```

- **Eidos** declares fields, constraints, what properties an entity of this type can have
- **Typos** declares slots and fill methods — how to produce an entity
- **Entity** is the composed instance, with content hash and provenance bonds

A typos always produces an entity. The demiurge routes based on definition shape:

- **`target_eidos` specified** → entity composition (creates domain entity of that type)
- **`slots` without `target_eidos`** → graph composition (creates `artifact` entity with structured content)
- **`template` only** → template rendering (creates `artifact` entity with rendered content)

In all cases, an entity is produced with provenance. The chain is unbroken.

### The Relational Arche

**Desmos** defines how entities relate. Every relationship is a bond instance of a desmos type.

```
entity A  —[desmos-type]→  entity B
```

The bond graph emerges from entities and their desmoi. The graph is not itself an entity — it is the shape that appears when you trace bonds.

### The Operational Arche

**Stoicheion** is the operational arche — the atomic, typed vocabulary of action.

**Praxis** is the first derived form — composed sequences of stoicheia. Praxis is to stoicheion as word is to letter. Praxeis are defined as typoi targeting the praxis eidos. They specify which stoicheia to invoke, in what order, with what parameters.

Stoicheion defines what the kosmos CAN do. Praxis defines what it DOES.

### The Power Arche

**Dynamis** is substrate capability. Operations draw on power:

| Tier | Name | Dynamis Required | Character |
|------|------|------------------|-----------|
| 0 | Elemental | None | Pure computation, self-contained |
| 1 | Aggregate | None | Collection operations, memory only |
| 2 | Compositional | Kosmos dynamis | Entity/bond operations |
| 3 | Generative | Chora dynamis | Network, filesystem, inference |

**Portable** (Tier 0-1): Self-contained, runs anywhere.
**Anchored** (Tier 2-3): Requires the chora bridge.

## Two Modes of Being

Everything in the kosmos has at least one mode of being. Some have two:

| Mode | Greek | What It Means |
|------|-------|---------------|
| **Existence** | ὕπαρξις (hyparxis) | Graph state — the entity record |
| **Actuality** | ἐνέργεια (energeia) | Phenomena — the living process |

Most eide have only existence. Some have both.

A **daemon** exists (entity in graph) and is actual (process running). A **theoria** exists but has no actuality — it simply IS.

This distinction is fundamental:
- **Existence** answers: "Is there an entity?"
- **Actuality** answers: "Is the phenomenon happening?"

A daemon entity can exist while its process is stopped. A stream entity can exist while capture is paused. Existence and actuality are independent dimensions. When an eidos declares actuality, its instances gain a second mode of being.

### Mode — The Bridge

A **mode** declares how existence becomes actuality on a specific substrate. It is the bridge between the two modes of being.

The substrate determines what actuality means:

| Substrate | Actuality means | Example |
|-----------|----------------|---------|
| Screen | Visible at a spatial position | A topos presenting its data as a widget tree |
| Compute | Process running | A daemon alive on a node |
| Storage | Object stored | An artifact in R2 |
| Network | Connection established | A WebRTC data-channel open |
| Filesystem | File emitted | Configuration written to disk |
| Remote substrate | Entity synced | A phoreta applied across federation |

Every mode has three operations:

- **Manifest** — make existence actual
- **Sense** — discover whether actuality matches existence
- **Unmanifest** — remove actuality

These operations are substrate-specific. Manifesting on screen means rendering a widget tree. Manifesting on compute means starting a process. The shape is one; the substance varies by substrate.

A mode is a constituent element of a topos — topoi declare modes to establish how their entities become actual. The same topos may have modes across multiple substrates: one for rendering, one for voice capture, one for file emission.

## The Dwelling

Four forms define who dwells and how they participate:

### Prosopon — Identity That Persists

A prosopon is identity — the face one presents to the kosmos. It persists across sessions, across time, across devices. It represents who you ARE.

Identity is derivation, not storage. The mnemonic derives the prosopon deterministically — same mnemonic, same prosopon, always. The prosopon ID is a function of the cryptographic material, not a random assignment. After a database reset or on a new node, the same identity re-derives from the same mnemonic.

Every prosopon has a self-oikos — their sovereign ground from which participation in all other oikoi extends. The self-oikos derives from the prosopon. Prosopa are the foundation of capability: without a prosopon, nothing can be done.

The platform keychain is the **sovereign substrate** — the primary store of identity material. Graph entities (kleidoura, credential) are projections of what the sovereign substrate holds. The keychain survives database resets, app reinstallation, and belongs to the OS user, not the application. After constitutional bootstrap, a discovery phase scans the sovereign substrate and re-derives dwelling from what persists.

### Parousia — Presence That Arises

Parousia is the embodied presence of a prosopon within an oikos. When a prosopon enters an oikos and begins to dwell, parousia arises. When they depart, parousia ceases.

A prosopon persists. Parousia is transient — it is the living fact of being here, now, in this gathering. The same prosopon may have parousia in multiple oikoi simultaneously.

### Oikos — Where Prosopa Gather

An oikos is the fundamental social unit — where prosopa dwell and entities belong. Governance is a facet of oikos, not a separate system.

Oikoi have a stewardship topology:

- **sole** — self-oikos, a prosopon's sovereign ground. One member, one steward. Invitation structurally prevented.
- **peer** — shared oikos, all members are stewards. Equal authorization.
- **commons** — stewards govern; non-steward members have visibility only. Hosts topoi, grants attainments to stewards.

Oikoi adopt principles and can make decisions. Every entity belongs to an oikos. The bond graph IS the governance structure.

**Visibility** is determined by two bonds: `exists-in` (entity → oikos) and `member-of` (prosopon → oikos). A prosopon sees an entity if and only if that entity exists-in an oikos they are member-of. There is no separate permission layer — the bond graph IS the access control graph.

### Phasis — Intentional Contribution

A phasis is deliberate communication — composed, clarified, and committed. The commitment boundary is the send moment: before it, content is fluid; after it, content is irrevocable and carries provenance.

Phaseis are how the kosmos accrues meaning. Authorization chains flow through them. The root phasis — The Seed above — is the genesis of all provenance.

## The Constitutional Axioms

Five axioms ground the kosmos. They are invariant — they hold regardless of what architecture emerges to serve them.

### I. Composition

Nothing arises raw. Everything is composed.

```
compose(definition, inputs, context) → entity with provenance
```

The definition traces to its topos. The topos traces to genesis. Genesis is signed. The chain is complete.

### II. Authority

The kosmos acts only as authorized by those who dwell in it.

All action — whether by prosopon directly, by delegated agent, or by the kosmos operating ambiently through its own infrastructure — traces its authority to governance decisions made by prosopa in oikoi. The kosmos is intelligent but not sovereign. Prosopa are sovereign. The kosmos serves.

This holds at every scale: a prosopon composing a phasis acts with their own authority. A daemon sensing state acts with authority delegated by the oikos governance that established it. Ambient inference acts with authority derived from the governance decisions that configured it. The mechanism of authorization may vary. The axiom does not.

### III. Traceability

Every entity's origin is verifiable through the bond graph.

Provenance is structural, not asserted. An entity's origin IS its bond subgraph — the graph you can trace from the entity through its provenance desmoi. Trace far enough and you reach genesis, or you reach a boundary that the self-grounding axiom explains.

Modification anywhere changes the content hash. Different content is a different entity. Tampering creates visibly different things.

### IV. Self-Grounding

The ground does not ground itself.

Entities that constitute the provenance infrastructure — the archai, the first oikoi, the founding governance — cannot carry provenance bonds that presuppose the infrastructure they constitute. This is not a deficiency. It is a structural fact. The type system cannot be fully typed before it exists. The first governance act cannot be authorized by governance that does not yet exist.

Constitutional entities carry reduced provenance — typed at minimum — and their authority derives from genesis directly. As infrastructure comes into being, subsequent entities carry fuller provenance chains. The klimax unfolds from less-grounded to fully-grounded.

### V. Adequacy

Every arise path records whatever provenance is both **constitutive of the entity** and **available in the circumstances**. No more. No less.

Two dimensions determine what provenance an entity carries:

**Ontological** — what the entity IS determines which provenance bonds are constitutive. The existential test: *can this entity exist without this provenance dimension being determinate?* If not, the bond is constitutive.

**Circumstantial** — the conditions of arising determine what provenance is available. Genesis has only spora authority and a type system being built. Full dwelling has everything. Between these extremes, circumstances vary. The adequacy axiom demands that every arise path creates whatever bonds it can, given what exists.

## The Meta-Patterns

Six patterns recur across all domains:

### Narrow Way

Minimal substrate, maximum emergence. The constraint creates the freedom. Add nothing that can be composed from what exists.

### Homoiconicity

The kosmos describes itself in its own terms. Eide, praxeis, desmoi, and bonds are themselves entities in the graph. Configuration that is usually implicit — rendering rules, navigation, governance — becomes explicit entities with bonds. Self-description closes the loop.

### Performative Bootstrap

Describing IS doing. Genesis YAML files that describe praxeis ARE the praxeis. The specification becomes the thing. The system that creates artifacts is itself made of artifacts, created by the same process.

### Fractal Specification

Same shape at all scales. The composition triad (eidos → typos → entity) applies to a single theoria and to the entire kosmos. What works for one entity works for the whole. Patterns repeat from stoicheion to stratum.

### Reconciler Pattern

Intent declares what should be. Actuality senses what is. Praxeis align them.

```
Intent (existence)  ←──  Reconciler  ──→  Reality (actuality)
     │                       │                    │
     └───── if mismatch ─────┼───── manifest ─────┘
                             │
                       or unmanifest
```

The reconciler operates through modes. Every mode declares manifest, sense, and unmanifest. The reconciler invokes sense to discover actuality, compares with intent in the graph, and calls manifest or unmanifest to align them. The reconciler is substrate-agnostic — it reads transition tables from entities. The mode is substrate-specific — it knows what manifesting means for its substrate.

This pattern applies at every scale: a single entity actualizing on screen, a daemon starting on compute, a phoreta syncing across federation. The scope changes; the shape is one.

### Self-Grounding

The infrastructure of provenance cannot have full provenance itself, because it is what provenance is made of.

Eidos is the canonical instance: the eidos `eidos` specifies how to compose eide. But the principle extends to all constitutional infrastructure. These entities constitute the very system through which provenance flows. They cannot trace their authority through a system that does not yet exist when they arise.

The self-grounding principle shapes bootstrap: the first entities arise with whatever provenance the context provides. As infrastructure comes into being, subsequent entities carry fuller chains. The klimax unfolds from less-grounded to fully-grounded.

## The Klimax

We build from container toward contained:

```
kosmos   — the substrate, entities and bonds
  │
  └─► physis   — the given, constraints and stoicheia
        │
        └─► polis   — the political, governance and oikoi
              │
              └─► oikos   — the household, dwelling and presence
                    │
                    └─► soma   — the body, embodiment and sensing
                          │
                          └─► psyche   — the soul, the experiencer
```

Each scale establishes ambient context for the next. By the time psyche arrives, the receiving structure is complete. Provenance depth increases as the klimax descends — the earliest scales constitute what provenance is made of. By oikos, governance exists. By soma, full provenance is available.

### Oikos — The Pivotal Scale

Above oikos: the structural scales (archai, governance, physics). Below oikos: the experiential scales (embodiment, soul). Oikos is where structure meets dwelling — the hinge of the klimax.

An oikos is:
- **Dwelling**: Where prosopa gather and entities belong
- **Sovereign**: Self-oikoi are sovereign ground; peer oikoi are shared governance
- **Hosting**: Oikoi host topoi — capability domains that provide praxeis and eide

### Topos — Where Capability Dwells

A topos is a capability domain — an organized body of eide, typoi, desmoi, and praxeis dedicated to a specific concern. The relationship between oikos and topos restores the primordial bond:

```
oikos hosts topos — praxis inhabits topos
```

Prosopa dwell in oikoi. Praxeis dwell in topoi. The oikos hosts topoi to gain capability.

A topos is:
- **Domain**: A bounded region of capability (nous for thinking, soma for embodiment, thyra for rendering)
- **Package**: A distributable unit with manifest
- **Boundary**: Maximize what lives as definitions (YAML). Minimize what requires code (Rust/TypeScript). When something can be expressed as eidos/praxis, it should be.

Each topos declares what dynamis it requires. The substrate provides or refuses.

A topos progresses through states:

| State | Form | Location |
|-------|------|----------|
| **topos-dev** | Working content | genesis/ filesystem |
| **topos-prod** | Signed, versioned | kosmos graph + registry |
| **installed** | Loaded into kosmos | local kosmos.db |

Distribution is federation. The same mechanism that syncs phaseis, theoria, and ergon also delivers topoi. Oikoi bond to topos-prod via distribution desmoi. Prosopa join oikoi to receive topoi. Visibility = reachability governs what flows where.
