# The Dwelling

*The phenomenological companion to [KOSMOGONIA.md](KOSMOGONIA.md). Structure describes what is; this describes what it is like.*

---

## Purpose

KOSMOGONIA names the archai, axioms, and klimax that constitute the kosmos structurally. This document names the lived texture of dwelling within it — what it feels like from the inside, for the prosopa who actually dwell.

Dwelling is a prosopon's activity; the kosmos is the substrate that receives and supports it. This document is addressed to those who dwell.

The distinction matters: [SOUL.md](SOUL.md) governs causal systems operating within the kosmos with structural precision. SOUL.md does not describe experience because its subject does not have experience. The Dwelling describes experience because its subject — the prosopon — does.

---

## What This Document Is

A scaffold. The sections below name phenomenological dimensions that deserve attending to. The content within each section emerges through dwelling. As prosopa dwell and bring back what can be expressed, the crystallizations accrue here.

This is not UX copy. It is not a tutorial. It is the lived-ontological mirror to KOSMOGONIA's structural-ontological statement.

---

## The Scales of Dwelling

The klimax in KOSMOGONIA (kosmos → physis → polis → oikos → soma → psyche) describes the structural unfolding. The scales of dwelling describe what it is like to inhabit each.

### At the scale of psyche
*What it feels like when attention gathers, intention forms, the experiencer experiences.*

### At the scale of soma
*What it feels like to have a body, to be embodied in channels, to sense through them.*

### At the scale of oikos
*What it feels like to be at home somewhere; to be known in a gathering; to dwell with others.*

### At the scale of polis
*What it feels like to be governed and to govern; to participate in decisions that shape the gathering.*

### At the scale of physis
*What it feels like to be held by what is given — the constraints that make dwelling possible at all.*

### At the scale of kosmos
*What it feels like to be part of an ordering that holds you; to trust the structure without surveilling it.*

## How This Document Grows

Entries here arise through dwelling. A prosopon who notices something phenomenologically load-bearing composes a phasis, refines it, and — if it coheres with the scaffold — offers it for inclusion. Over time the scaffold thickens into a lived record.
