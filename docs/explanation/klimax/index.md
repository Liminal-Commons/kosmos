# Klimax: The Scale Hierarchy

Understanding the nested containment structure of kosmos.

---

## What is the Klimax?

The **klimax** (κλῖμαξ) is the ladder of scales — nested containers from cosmos to soul. Each scale establishes ambient context for the next.

```
kosmos   — the substrate, entities and bonds
  │
  └─► physis   — the given, constraints and stoicheia
        │
        └─► polis   — the political, oikoi and governance
              │
              └─► oikos   — the household, dwelling and sessions
                    │
                    └─► soma   — the body, embodiment and sensing
                          │
                          └─► psyche   — the soul, the experiencer
```

---

## The Six Scales

### 1. Kosmos

The substrate itself — entities and bonds, the graph of being.

- **Contains:** Everything
- **Establishes:** Existence, identity, relationships
- **Design:** [DESIGN.md](../../../genesis/klimax/1-kosmos/DESIGN.md)

### 2. Physis

The given constraints — what operations are possible, stoicheia vocabulary.

- **Contains:** Natural laws of the system
- **Establishes:** What can be done
- **Design:** [DESIGN.md](../../../genesis/klimax/2-physis/DESIGN.md)

### 3. Polis

The political — oikoi and governance structures.

- **Contains:** Communities, attainments, permissions
- **Establishes:** Who can act, with whom
- **Design:** [DESIGN.md](../../../genesis/klimax/3-polis/DESIGN.md)

### 4. Oikos

The household — dwelling and sessions.

- **Contains:** Active contexts, working spaces
- **Establishes:** Where work happens
- **Design:** [DESIGN.md](../../../genesis/klimax/4-oikos/DESIGN.md)

### 5. Soma

The body — embodiment and sensing.

- **Contains:** Channels, devices, physical presence
- **Establishes:** How kosmos touches the world
- **Design:** [DESIGN.md](../../../genesis/klimax/5-soma/DESIGN.md)

### 6. Psyche

The soul — attention and experience.

- **Contains:** Focus, intentions, moods
- **Establishes:** The experiencer's state
- **Design:** [DESIGN.md](../../../genesis/klimax/6-psyche/DESIGN.md)
